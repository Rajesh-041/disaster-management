# Design Specification: Disaster Management & Resilience Platform (Flash Rescue)

This design specification adapts a serene, organic, and highly structured layout into a reassuring, user-friendly, and accessible Disaster Management Platform. The goal is to provide calming clarity, intuitive resource distribution, and clear calls to action during high-stress situations while completely transitioning the legacy static site architecture into a centralized component system[cite: 1].

---

## 1. Design Philosophy & Visual Identity

### 1.1 Aesthetic Overview
*   **Tone:** Reassuring, dependable, structured, and empathetic. 
*   **Goal:** To counter the anxiety of disaster scenarios with a clean, grounded layout that emphasizes safety, immediate support, and clear pathways to recovery.

### 1.2 Color Palette
Instead of high-anxiety stark blacks, deep navies, and alarming neon highlights, this palette shifts to empathetic tokens that establish structure, reassurance, and readable clarity[cite: 1].

| Token Name | Hex Value | Design Application |
|---|---|---|
| `bg-primary` | `#F4F7F5` / `#F9F6F0` | Light Sage / Soft Sand background to decrease visual fatigue[cite: 1]. |
| `text-primary` | `#162E27` | Deep Midnight Teal for heavy body font, navigation text, and structural headers[cite: 1]. |
| `accent-warm` | `#D4A373` | Muted Ochre for non-emergency CTAs, informational boundaries, and active states[cite: 1]. |
| `safe-green` | `#3A5F43` | Grounded Forest Green indicating reliable, secure areas or active resources[cite: 1]. |
| `alert-soft` | `#E07A5F` | Soft Terracotta / Rust reserved exclusively for active warnings (avoiding neon red)[cite: 1]. |
| `surface` | `#FFFFFF` | Clear, elevated white canvas containers for cards, forms, and updates[cite: 1]. |

### 1.3 Typography
*   **Headings (H1, H2, H3):** *Playfair Display* or *Georgia* (Serif)[cite: 1]. Offers a deeply reassuring, authoritative, yet gentle appearance for core sections and calming welcome notes[cite: 1].
*   **Body Text:** *Inter* or *Open Sans* (Sans-Serif)[cite: 1]. Highly readable in high-stress or low-bandwidth situations[cite: 1].
